INTRODUCTION

A Special Journey for Special Minds. We are proud to launch our International Summer Camp 2025, hosted by Fit 5 Sports Club | India, exclusively designed for children and youth with Intellectual or Learning Disabilities from around the globe.

Adventure and Activities

Camp Life & Experiences: A Journey of Culture, Nature, Wellness, and Growth

Discover a thoughtfully designed blend of cultural exposure, nature-based learning, wellness activities, and life skills—crafted to nurture every camper’s mind, body, and spirit. From tribal interactions to therapy sessions, star gazing to social service, every experience is curated to inspire confidence, creativity, and connection.

Cultural & Enrichment Activities
  • ⬥ Natural Farm Picking: Campers explore organic farm plots, picking fruits or vegetables—a hands-on experience that teaches nature appreciation and healthy eating.
  • ⬥ Meet the Tribes: Guided interactions with local tribal communities around Madhya Pradesh to share stories, traditional art, and cultural performances, fostering respect and curiosity.
  • ⬥ Hawan (Om Chanting) Ceremony: Daily group chanting and fire ritual to promote mindfulness, peace, and a sense of togetherness in a calm, spiritual setting.
  • ⬥ Star Gazing & Night Campfire: Learn about constellations with guided stargazing sessions followed by music and storytelling around a safe, supervised campfire.
  • ⬥ Walk Without Shoes: Earthing walks across grassy lawns or sand pits to ground campers, stimulating sensory awareness and connection with the earth.
Health & Wellness
  • ⬥ Therapy Sessions: On-site physiotherapy and occupational therapy tailored to individual needs to support motor skill development.
  • ⬥ Health Insurance Coverage: Comprehensive medical insurance is included to ensure well-being and peace of mind for all participants.
  • ⬥ Traditional Herbal Demonstrations: Introductory workshops on Ashwagandha, Brahmi, and other Indian herbs—explaining their benefits and safe uses.
Adventure & Excursions
  • ⬥ Waterfall Visit: A day trip to a nearby waterfall (e.g., Madhav National Park) for gentle hikes and refreshing outdoor play in a beautiful natural setting.
  • ⬥ Mud House Overnight Stay: Optional immersive experience sleeping in eco-friendly mud huts—teaching about sustainable living and rural crafts.
  • ⬥ Plantation Activity: Campers plant saplings on-site, learning environmental stewardship and leaving a green legacy behind.
Logistics & Support
  • ⬥ Airport Pickup & Drop: Seamless transportation for outstation guests to/from Delhi Airport or railway station, ensuring smooth arrival and departure.
  • ⬥ Missing Item Arrangement: Lost-and-found support, including replacement of essential items to ensure campers feel secure and cared for.
  • ⬥ Emergency Contacts & Support: 24/7 access to medical staff and emergency contact numbers provided at check-in.
Community & Skill Building
  • ⬥ Sign Language Expert Support: Certified instructors teach basic Indian Sign Language (ISL), helping campers communicate and learn empathy.
  • ⬥ Volunteering & Social Service: Guided volunteer projects in local Gwalior communities—like planting trees or book drives—to foster social responsibility.
  • ⬥ Picture Book Activities: Campers create illustrated storybooks to reflect on their experience, encouraging creativity and language development.
Food & Nutrition
  • ⬥ Variety of Meal Options: Balanced vegetarian meals, healthy snacks, and hydration stations—catering to diverse tastes and dietary needs.
  • ⬥ Packed Drinking Water: Recycled, eco-safe water bottles provided daily, ensuring constant hydration and reducing plastic use.
Explore Beyond the Camp
  • ⬥ Jaipur, Pachmarhi, Khajuraho & More Tours: Educational trips to these iconic destinations—featuring cultural landmarks, temples, and mountain landscapes.
